Why I chose BYU

I love being a student at BYU. There are so many things about campus and the institution itself that provide an environment for me to feel at home away from home. BYU provides a place where the gospel can be felt and lived in all areas through devotionals, prayer, service opportunities, and more. One of the reasons I wanted to come to BYU was because of the Ballroom Dance program. BYU has the top college ballroom dance program in the country and it shows. I love being able to continue to learn the art I grew up with and to be blessed to share it with others. Check out BYU Ballroom to learn more about the programs available!
